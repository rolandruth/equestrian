About CES 2027

CES is the most powerful tech event on the planet — the single stage where the world's most influential companies come to declare what the next 12 months will look like. Every January, Las Vegas becomes the center of gravity for the global technology industry, pulling in 142,000 professionals from 160 countries across AI, automotive, health tech, robotics, smart home, quantum computing, and 40+ product categories. What happens at CES does not stay in Las Vegas — the announcements made on these floors move stock prices, reshape supply chains, and set the product roadmap for every major consumer brand on Earth. From Jensen Huang's legendary keynotes that define the AI era, to the Eureka Park startup pavilion where the next billion-dollar company is pitching from a 10x10 booth, CES is where the future stops being a concept and becomes a product you can touch. Whether you are a Fortune 500 executive scouting competitive intelligence, an engineer hunting the cutting edge, a founder seeking distribution partners, or a journalist covering the story of the decade — CES 2027 is the one event you cannot afford to miss. Registration is free for qualified industry professionals, and the show floor spans over 2.5 million square feet across the Las Vegas Convention Center, Venetian Expo, and Resorts World — plan for 10-mile days and bring comfortable shoes.

Venue & Location

Las Vegas Convention Center
3150 Paradise Rd, Las Vegas, NV 89109
✈️
By Air
Harry Reid International Airport (LAS) — 5 miles from LVCC. Average $25–35 Uber/Lyft. Taxi service available. Official CES airport shuttles run Jan 5–9.
🚌
Shuttle Service
Free CES shuttles connect LVCC, Venetian Expo, Resorts World, and 40+ official hotels every 10–15 minutes, 7am–8pm daily throughout the event.
🚈
Rail / Monorail
Las Vegas Monorail: Convention Center station (LVCC North Hall entrance). $5/ride or $25/day unlimited pass. Connects to MGM Grand, Bally's, and other Strip hotels.
🅿️
Parking
LVCC Parking Lots C, D, G: $10–15/day. Limited availability — arrive early or use rideshare. Rideshare pickup/dropoff at designated zones on Paradise Rd.
